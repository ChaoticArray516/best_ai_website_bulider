## Our Testing Process

Every AI website builder we review goes through a standardized testing process. We evaluate each platform across four key dimensions to ensure fair and consistent comparisons.

## Scoring Criteria

### 1. Ease of Use (30%)
We measure how quickly a complete beginner can go from sign-up to a published website. We time the process, count the number of steps, and evaluate the quality of AI-generated output on the first attempt.

### 2. Features (30%)
We evaluate the breadth and depth of features including AI capabilities, template quality, ecommerce support, integrations, and CMS functionality.

### 3. Pricing & Value (20%)
We assess the pricing structure, free plan limitations, and whether the paid plans offer good value relative to competitors.

### 4. SEO Friendliness (20%)
We check for built-in SEO tools, clean HTML output, Core Web Vitals performance, and mobile responsiveness.

## Independence Policy

Our editorial team makes all scoring decisions independently. Affiliate relationships do not influence review scores or rankings. We disclose all affiliate relationships transparently.
