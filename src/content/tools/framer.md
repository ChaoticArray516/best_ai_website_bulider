## What is Framer?

Framer started as a prototyping tool and has evolved into a powerful website builder that bridges the gap between design and development. With AI features, Framer can now generate layouts from text descriptions while maintaining its signature design flexibility. It's the tool of choice for designers who want creative control.

## Key Features Overview

Framer's AI helps with initial layout generation and content creation, but the real power lies in its visual editor. Users can control every pixel of their design while leveraging AI for repetitive tasks. The platform also supports React components for developers who want to add custom functionality.
