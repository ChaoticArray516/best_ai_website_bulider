## What is Squarespace AI?

Squarespace has long been the go-to choice for creatives who demand beautiful design. With the addition of AI features, Squarespace now helps users generate content, optimize their sites, and create brand assets faster than ever. The platform maintains its reputation for stunning templates while adding intelligent automation.

## Key Features Overview

Squarespace AI focuses on content generation and design assistance rather than full site automation. Users select from curated templates and use AI to populate content, generate blog ideas, and create marketing materials. The result is a polished, professional website that stands out from generic AI-generated designs.
