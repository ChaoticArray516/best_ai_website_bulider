## What is 10Web?

10Web combines the power of WordPress with AI automation. It generates complete WordPress websites using AI, then hosts them on Google Cloud with automated optimization. For users who want the flexibility of WordPress without the technical headaches, 10Web offers an attractive middle ground.

## Key Features Overview

The platform's AI generates WordPress sites based on user input, complete with themes, plugins, and content. 10Web then handles hosting, security, backups, and performance optimization automatically. Users get the world's most popular CMS with the simplicity of a modern website builder.
