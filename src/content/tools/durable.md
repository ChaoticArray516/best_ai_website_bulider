## What is Durable?

Durable is a new generation AI website builder focused on speed and simplicity. It targets small local businesses that need an online presence without any technical knowledge. The platform goes beyond websites, offering built-in tools for customer management, invoicing, and online marketing.

## Key Features Overview

Durable's AI engine generates a complete business website in 30 seconds using just a business name and industry. The platform includes CRM functionality to track leads, an AI invoice generator, and automated Google Business Profile syncing. It's an all-in-one business toolkit rather than just a website builder.
