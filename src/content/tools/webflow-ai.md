## What is Webflow AI?

Webflow has long been the choice of professional designers who need visual development power. With the addition of AI Assist, Webflow now helps users generate and edit content, making the platform more accessible while maintaining its professional-grade capabilities. It's the go-to tool for complex, custom websites.

## Key Features Overview

Webflow AI assists with content generation and editing within the visual editor. Users can generate copy, translate content, and get design suggestions. The core Webflow experience remains unchanged — full visual control over HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — but with AI assistance to speed up workflows.
