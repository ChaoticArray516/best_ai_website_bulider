## What is Wix AI?

Wix AI is the artificial intelligence layer built into the Wix website builder platform. It uses advanced machine learning algorithms to generate complete websites from a simple text prompt. Users describe their business or idea, and Wix AI creates a fully functional site with relevant content, images, and layout in under a minute.

## Key Features Overview

The platform combines traditional website building with AI automation. Users can start with AI generation and then fine-tune every aspect using Wix's drag-and-drop editor. This hybrid approach gives beginners speed while still offering advanced customization for growing businesses.
